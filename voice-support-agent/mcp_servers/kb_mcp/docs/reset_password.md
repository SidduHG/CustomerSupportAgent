# Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it yourself from the sign-in page. Click the "Forgot password?" link below the password field. Enter the email address associated with your account and we will send a reset link within a few minutes. The reset link expires after 30 minutes for security.

# Password Requirements

Your new password must be at least 12 characters long and include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. You cannot reuse any of your last five passwords. If your new password is rejected, error code PW-204 means it does not meet these requirements.

# Didn't Receive the Reset Email

If the reset email has not arrived after ten minutes, check your spam or junk folder. Make sure you entered the same email address you used to register. If you still do not see it, the address may not be registered, and you should contact support to verify your account.
