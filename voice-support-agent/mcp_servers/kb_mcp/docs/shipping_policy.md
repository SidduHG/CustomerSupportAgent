# Shipping Times

Standard shipping takes three to five business days within the continental United States. Expedited shipping arrives in one to two business days. Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern Time ship the same day; orders after that ship the next business day.

# International Shipping

We ship to most countries. International orders take seven to fourteen business days depending on the destination and customs processing. Any import duties or taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the shipping cost.

# Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships you will receive a tracking number by email. You can also find tracking under Orders in your account. If tracking has not updated in more than three business days, the carrier may have a delay, and you should contact support with your order number.

# Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged or does not arrive within the expected window, contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will send a replacement or issue a refund. Claims filed after 14 days may not be eligible.
