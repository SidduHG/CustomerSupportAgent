# Refunds

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically take five to seven business days to appear on your statement. To request a refund, go to Billing, select the order, and choose "Request refund".

# Subscription Billing

Subscriptions renew automatically on the same calendar day each month. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from the Billing page. Annual plans are billed once per year and include a 20 percent discount compared to paying monthly.

# Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel anytime from the Billing page; your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period and is not prorated. After cancellation you keep access to paid features until that period ends. If you are charged after cancelling, error code BL-409 indicates the cancellation did not complete, and you should contact support for a reversal.

# Updating Payment Method

To update your card, go to Billing and select "Payment method". We accept all major credit cards and debit cards. A failed payment is retried automatically for three days before the account is suspended.
